Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Agentic Fixer?

Agentic Fixer is a tool that analyzes web pages for agent-readiness issues and provides stack-specific developer fixes.

How does it work?

Simply enter your URL and select your target stack. The tool will analyze your page and provide actionable fixes.

Is it free to use?

Yes, Agentic Fixer is free to use for analyzing your web pages.

Technical Questions

What stacks are supported?

We currently support Next.js 13 App Router, React SPA, and Plain HTML.

What issues can it detect?

It can detect missing structured data, policy surface issues, heading structure problems, and more.
